enum air_quality_status_t

Return codes for the status of air quality index function.

Enumerator
EXCELLENT 

The air inside is as fresh as the air outside.

FINE 

The air quality inside remains at harmless levels.

MODERATE 

The air quality inside has reached conspicuous levels.

POOR 

The air quality inside has reached precarious levels.

VERY_POOR 

The air quality inside has reached unacceptable levels.

SEVERE 

The air quality inside has exceeded maximum workplace concentration values.
